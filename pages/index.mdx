# Introduction
 
Label Baker is a web application that allows users to create custom labels for various purposes, such as labeling products, organizing documents, or identifying items in a collection. This application provides a variety of templates, layouts, and design tools to help users customize the appearance of their labels, including options for text, graphics, shapes, barcodes, QRcode, and more. Users can also import their own images or logos to use on the labels. Once the labels are designed, they can be printed out on a variety of materials, such as paper, vinyl, or plastic. Label Baker also offers automatic label generation based on data input or integration with external data sources.

# Core Features

## A wide range of templates and design tools 
LabelBaker offers a variety of templates and design tools to help users customize the appearance of their labels. Users can choose from different font styles, sizes, and colors, as well as add graphics and images to their labels.

## Importing and exporting options 
LabelBaker allows you to import and export template files. Once you have made a template you can export it for later use.

## Layout Maker
Built in tool that helps you to make your own layout.

## Automatic label generation 
LabelBaker can generate labels automatically based on data input, such as product names or serial numbers.

## Integration with external databases
You can import xls / csv data as your data source. You can also import data from external API.

## Woocommerce 
You can make labels for your woocommerce orders and products.

## Multilingual
LabelBaker is available in 8 languages ( English, Bangla, Arabic, German, French, Chinese, Hebrew, and Spanish).
